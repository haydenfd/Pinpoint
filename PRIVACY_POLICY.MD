# Privacy Policy

Pinpoint does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

## Data Handling
Pinpoint allows users to bookmark responses from AI chat interfaces. All bookmarked content is stored locally in the user’s browser using Chrome’s local storage APIs. No data is transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.

## Permissions
Pinpoint only accesses supported AI chat websites in order to provide bookmarking functionality. The extension does not track users, monitor browsing activity, or collect analytics.

## Data Sharing
Pinpoint does not share user data with third parties.

## Changes
This privacy policy may be updated if the extension’s functionality changes.
